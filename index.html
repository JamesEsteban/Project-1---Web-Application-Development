About Us

Burger Boss is a local, family-run burger restaurant situated in the heart of the UK. We take great pride in our usage of the finest ingredients and in crafting the most delectable burgers. Our cuisine is always fresh, substantial, and gratifying. Consensus dictates that our burgers are unrivaled in the area! We readily accommodate bookings of all sizes and are well-equipped to cater to your parties and events.

Our chef is perpetually conjuring up new and exhilarating burgers, which we showcase on a daily specials board. You have the opportunity to contribute your own recipes to the specials leaderboard, where you can cast your vote for your preferred creation. At the end of each month, the winning burger will be incorporated into our menu! Our convivial ambience and mouthwatering burgers create the perfect synergy!
